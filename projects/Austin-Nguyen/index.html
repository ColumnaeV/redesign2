Personal Introductory

I’ve got quite the spectrum of interests as seen in the main title section above, so I’ll keep this part very quick and short. First I’d like to introduce myself to you. My name is Austin Nguyen and currently I — when I created this — am a senior at Millard North High School. I love to expose myself to what the world has to offer because I seek its plethora of entertainment. Being moderately social, I wouldn’t mind to share a dance or a cup of Dunkin' Donuts coffee.
