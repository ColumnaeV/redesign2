The Afterword

The first thing I would like to mention is how awesome this IB class is. All of you have not only made a huge impact on me, but also those who you have surrounded yourself with. We’ve all have had our ups and downs with each other, but nothing has been able to separate us. Each day we grew stronger into a unified community that most of our authoritative figures would expect (Daughty lol). Personally, this was the family that I was looking for since the beginning of high school and I’m glad that I’ve got to meet every single one of you. I can pick out a single moment in the past two years we spent together that can highlight a way in which you have changed my way of living for all of you. I believe that each and every single one of you deserve more than just a superlative for I know that you all will be even more successful down the road. Enjoy laughs, share memories. This is the time in which you may look back upon and realise how great IB actually was (aside all suffering with school work...). Even though we may be spread all over the country world (because Tom is going back to Germany), I hope to meet up sometime in the future. Best wishes and always have a top of the morning.

Cheers,
Christian Son Nguyen
