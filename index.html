Learning More... About my Hobbies?

I've always been interested in cards for a very long time. I mean, most people have played with standard playing cards (four suites, thirteen values) at some point of their lives, but for me it was always about the patterns and manipulation of them. This soon led to my substantial collection of normal and designer quality playing cards as well as the art of card 'magic'. Although my performances of card flourishing and targeted attention diversion has drawn to a minimum, I still wish to continue this hobby whilst attending college.

Choice of Career Path?

Chemistry: the science which I find to be the most interesting. A degree in Chemistry is my foot in the door for medical school as to pursue the occupational field of medicine by becoming a doctor. Inspired by specific events of my youth, I found the desire to always help those in need. Caring for one's well-being has always been a high priority for me.

Were There Any Other Career Choices?

What very few know about me is that if I were to not pursue medicine, I would have followed through with becoming either an officer in the United States Marine Corps or a medic in the Navy to help those who are less fortunate due to war conflicts. From there, I would only be looking into Computer Science, specifically into frontend web development.

If Studying Chemistry & Medicine... Why Still Do Web Development?

Different styles of art have always caught my attention. Card flourishing was an expressive art style I found interest in (as mentioned before), but I was always limited to what my hands could physically do. Frontend coding however, is an unbounded art form that is limited to the creator's imagination. Every web project I make is simply a canvas of what I sought to be beautiful, which is something that I will probably never give up.
